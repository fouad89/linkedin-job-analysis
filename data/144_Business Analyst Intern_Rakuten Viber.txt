About the program: 
We're looking for talented, emerging talent to join the Viber team as part of our 2021 Global TEAM Internship program! As an intern, you will have the opportunity to work with experienced professionals globally. Our goal is to help you learn about the tech/messaging world, develop and refine your skills in the area of expertise. We've structured the program to provide each intern a TEAM, which consists of a Lead Mentor (manager), and two support Mentors to cater to the ever-changing landscape of WFH vs in-office. You'll be joined by interns from around the world!
Our internship is a 6-month program starting in July/Aug. We are looking forward to bringing on individuals who are finishing up their university studies, or recent graduates.

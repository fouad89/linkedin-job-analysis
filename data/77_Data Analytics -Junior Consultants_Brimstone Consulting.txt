Data Analytics Junior Consultants

Our client has a rapidly growing Technology department and we are currently seeking the right candidates for excellent career opportunities at Graduate and 2-3 years experience with the right skills to help that growth.

Working in our specialist forensic technology data analytics labs and onsite to help clients leverage knowledge from their data for measurement of fraud risks and losses, prevention, continuous monitoring, detection and investigation of occupational fraud, waste and abuse, financial crime and other forms of non-compliant activities including behavioural patterns
Working to support clients and fraud examiners on the benefits of forensic data analysis and how it can be applied to their issues

Role Objective

The Data Analyst is responsible for the consulting within our business to gather specification, design and analytical requirements for the build and delivery of feasibility prototypes, find efficiencies and interpret data to make recommendations. The role is an integral part of the Customer Excellence team, working across multiple business units to provide predictive and prescriptive analytics.

Key Responsibilities
Being the integral point of liaison between business stakeholders and team in Customer Excellence, responsible for the design and build of feasibility prototyping for specific business needs
Lead support on business deep dive analysis which reacts to the priority needs of senior leadership across multiple key brands, leveraging existing analytics solutions, platforms and data
Balances an innovative mindset with technical considerations throughout, building feasibility prototypes which in turn can be efficiently leveraged for wider systemization
Build Data Models and export views to enable Data Visualization
Responding to business requests for ad-hoc analysis and higher analytics, own the design, development, and maintenance of ongoing analysis in order to drive intelligent, well informed business decisions


Qualifications
Experience of programming languages (Python)
SQL and Python coding experience, building linear dimensional, regression-based and logistic models
Expert analytical and problem-solving skills, able to manage project timelines and deliverables
Excellent interpersonal skills with demonstrated ability to work effectively in multifunctional teams and with senior management
Ability to influence, partner, challenge thinking and gain credibility with internal stakeholders

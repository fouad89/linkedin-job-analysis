The role
We are seeking a Talent Data Analyst in our HR, Capability team on a 12 month fix term contract. The position will be based in our Bristol Head office, with your time being split between the office and remote working.
If you enjoy working in a fast-paced environment and you are looking to build a career in a dynamic FTSE100 FMCG company, with a focus on working within within HR Capability team with a focus on talent where no two days are the same this role could be your next step. We are looking for someone who has experience of dealing with large data sets and is comfortable with reporting their finding to the business and who enjoys working as part of a team.

Within your role as a Talent Data Analyst you are accountable for global Talent data, enabling the delivery of purposeful analysis that will support the Centre Of Expertise with identifying key risks and opportunities for the business. 

The ideal candidate is someone passionate about data, reporting and data storytelling. You will play a critical role in the maintenance of our current Talent database, importantly looking at how data analysis can enhance our approach to Talent Management across the globe. Furthermore you will help with the creation of Executive Committee presentations designed to inform business decisions and drive change.

This is an excellent opportunity for someone to make a real impact across a global function in the people arena.

Key accountabilities
Creating and delivering reports and presentations that will support markets and help to inform business decisions;
Engaging with relevant stakeholders to capture and prioritise data visualization requirements / storytelling;
Understand business and stakeholder needs enabling the identification of key trends and uncovering areas for improvement;
Work with other teams / global teams across the to identify opportunities to enrich the Talent data, reporting and analysis;
Conducting required maintenance duties to ensure our talent data is up to date / accurate. Producing regular presentations and reports as required;
Building guidance documents to upskill the HR community in the area of Talent Analytics;
Work with all areas of capability / markets to support ad hoc data and reporting requests as required;
Review best practice data collection and reporting solutions;
Collaborate with HR Operations to ensure data best practices are adopted including quality and efficient use of global sources;
Comply with GDPR requirements for all data reporting, analysis and sharing;
Support and contribute to the talent and overall capability agenda to develop understanding and add value;
Produce adhoc decks and documentation to support the capability agenda, working with different stakeholders as required.

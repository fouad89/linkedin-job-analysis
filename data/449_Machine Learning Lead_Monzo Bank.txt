Machine Learning Lead
Monzo Bank
London, England, United Kingdom
We're excited to be looking for a Lead Machine Learning Scientist to help build the bank of the future. You'll have the opportunity to lead the adoption and delivery of machine learning systems across entire areas of Monzo, working at the intersection of Data Science and Engineering, helping us to identify the best opportunities to pursue, and leading the specialist team.
The role

As Part Of This Role You'll
Be a key leader in building Machine Learning as a discipline; you will manage exceptional Machine Learning Scientists and help their career progression and development
Establish yourself as a trusted partner to leadership teams and build a strategic understanding of the business - while structuring complex projects to bring them to life, be it either hands-on or by leading others
Work with our Machine Learning Scientists and Backend Engineers to ensure that we capture the right data and build our systems with rigour
Be able to work on all aspects of machine learning as we operate as one team

You'll be embedded within our broader Data Science discipline at Monzo and our mission is to Enable Monzo to Make Better Decisions, Faster.

We have a strong culture of data-driven decision making across the whole company. And we're great believers in powerful, real-time analytics and empowerment of the wider business. We empower Machine Learning Scientists to work across the entire lifecycle of their models, including shipping the models they train into production.

We work across the entire company: Machine Learning Scientists alternate between working closely with cross-functional squads and investigating early ideas within the central machine learning team, all while being embedded into the broader Data Science discipline.

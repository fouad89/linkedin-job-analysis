About the role
You will form part of our newly created data team, supporting both internal projects and external client delivery. At first, you will mostly be focused on client delivery across several partner banks, however there is also internal product development work happening that you could get involved with if you want

As a Data Scientist at Jigsaw XYZ you will...
Partner with our clients, from data owners and users to C-level executives, to understand their needs and build impactful analytics solutions
Design and build machine learning solutions for use across our products and for clients
Use state of the art technologies to acquire, ingest and transform big datasets
Map data fields to hypothesis, curate, wrangle and prepare data to be used in advanced analytics models
Contribute to cross-functional problem-solving sessions with your team and deliver presentations to colleagues and clients
Be flexible to travel to our clients' offices to deliver presentations, gather information or share knowledge
Have the opportunity to contribute to R&D and internal asset development projects

The tech stack
Most of our data pipes are still being worked on, so you will help to define the tech stack. We are currently using Snowflake as our data warehouse, hosted on AWS. Most of our data work is carried out in SQL and Python, with a bit of PySpark.

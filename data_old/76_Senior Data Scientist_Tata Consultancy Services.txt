What we need 
We are looking for Senior Data Scientist for TCS Ireland.
 
Job Purpose and Primary Objectives:
The ideal person for this role will possess analytical prowess, problem-solving and management consulting skills.
An all-rounder who can think out of the box on daily basis and can help solve business problems with the help of data.
Majority of the role will involve problem understanding, data acquisition, data understanding, model development, model operationalization, stakeholder management and consulting.
 
Key Responsibilities:
Understand and manipulate large amounts of raw data into actionable insights.
Able to quickly experiment and identify hidden relationship among the data sources.
Identify new potential business opportunities while working on the project.
Build best in class solutions leveraging advanced machine learning and statistical algorithms.
Able to simplify and present the analytical insights to project stakeholders with analytical and non-analytical backgrounds.
Heavily focused on key development in the analytics research field and able to experiment and implement them in real project scenarios.
Work as a torch bearer for the junior members of the team
Able to take the ownership of multiple projects end to end without external supervision.
Manage projects plans and timelines to ensure that projects are delivered with the highest quality and on time.
Able to quantify the actual impacts of the projects delivered.
Build Data products in collaboration with other areas such as Visualization, Data Engineering, and IT.
 
Key Skills/Knowledge:
Expertise in statistical modelling techniques such as linear regression, logistic regression, generalized linear models, SVM, ensemble model (Random Forest, XGBoost), cluster analysis, PCA.
Expertise in the end-to-end model developments process

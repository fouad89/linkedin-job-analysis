Software Engineer I
Cerner Corporation
Dublin, County Dublin, Ireland
With a presence in the European healthcare IT markets spanning almost two decades, Cerner’s established solutions and industry expertise have placed our associates at the forefront of development and evolution in healthcare provision. Our continued growth and rapid expansion in key European markets provides opportunities for exciting careers in a dynamic environment – all focused on helping drive innovation in healthcare.

The Global Health Network team contributes IP development that helps shape Healthcare IT for all of Cerner's clients, but our main focus is the EMEA area.

Nevertheless, we provide updates and new features to multiple global offerings under the Population Health Management banner, specifically but not exclusively to Longitudinal Record, HealtheRecord, HealtheRegistries.

We are an Agile team of Software Engineers following a high-risk development process contributing to and supporting health information technology services on a global scale.

As a Software Engineer on the Global Health Network team you will be responsible for writing code, testing and debugging new software, making enhancements to existing software from clear requirements and participating in code reviews and identifying basic issues or deviations from best practices.

You will develop knowledge of assigned solution, technologies, development processes and ecosystem and provide support for existing solutions to consultants and technical solution analysts who handle direct client support.

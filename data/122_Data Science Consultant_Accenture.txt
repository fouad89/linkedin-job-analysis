As a team:

The Data Science Consultant will be part of Accenture Analytics as an Industry Data Scientist with the Industry Data Science Centre of Excellence, which works mainly in the engineering solutions area. They will be working with the team that identifies and develops advanced analytics solutions to engineering problems, using Engineering Knowledge, statistical models, machine learning methods and solutions for Accenture Resources clients to improve various business outcome indicators.

In our team you will learn:

Accenture is committed to the investment of its people, and the Global AI team is no exception. As a Data Science Consultant, you will have the opportunity to gain access to some of the brightest minds in the data science field, surrounded by peers who are involved in your future development.

You'll work on novel challenges in the industry and will expand your technical skills in creative and inventive ways, with the added benefit of improving your interpersonal and presentation skills by going on site with clients and communicating with a wide range of stakeholders.

Our data scientists spend time on the ground with engineers, whether that's in a mine, laboratories or on rigs, which offers you an industry experience like no other.

In this role you will:

Successfully develop, conceptualize and test various Engineering solutions, using statistical and machine learning models

Integrate the outcomes as real time analytics to elevate Accenture’s ability to create value for clients in areas and through means not immediately apparent to clients

Follow multiple approaches for project execution from adapting existing assets to Analytics use cases, exploring client data

Collaborate with other data scientists, subject matter experts, sales, and delivery teams to create solutions

Deliver strategic advanced analytics projects from design to execution.


We are looking for experience in the following skills:

Experience as a data scientist in relevant domains (Energy, Oil & Gas, Chemicals, Natural Resources, Mining & Metals, Utilities or Power) – with hands on experience handling data driven decisions in these areas or similar

Experience in at least one of the following – engineering related solutions, supervised and unsupervised learning, classification models, cluster analysis, neural networks, non-parametric methods, multivariate statistics, reliability models, Markov models, stochastic models, Bayesian models, deep learning, genetic algorithms, fuzzy logic or inference systems

Experience in various statistical and machine learning models, data mining and unstructured data analytics in corporate or academic research environments

Experience in statistical software (Python, R, Octave, Tensorflow)

Programming experience (Python, R or similar)


Set yourself apart:

As a Data Science Consultant we expect you to:

Solve engineering solutions using Statistical Methods, Machine Learning models

Think creatively to solve real world engineering business problems, which are not obvious, and use the clients extensive engineering knowledge and staff to develop with the clients, solutions that deliver increased efficiency, predict failures, etc.

Identify the key parameters that are involved in the solution and using the best algorithm to solve it

Work in a global collaborative environment where you will lead a small team

Developing and delivering machine learning models that work in an engineering production setting

Knowledge of UNIX or Linux environments

Work with sensor data coming of working assists, time series analysis, failure analysis using time series data

Work with large data streaming technologies like Spark

Work with relational databases and have an intermediate level knowledge of SQL

Proficient verbal and written communication skills in English

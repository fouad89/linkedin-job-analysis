LONDON

Massively successful insights consultancy is looking for a Junior Data Scientist to join their growing team. This company works with well-known clients across FMCG and retail, advising on buying patterns and customer behaviour. They're looking for a sparky individual with experience in SQL and Power BI, who is willing to learn new technologies and techniques within a fantastic data-driven team.

The Company

The company is a well-known insight consultancy, delivering high-quality data and measurement of the world's leading FMCG Brands and Retailers.

The Role

The Junior Data Scientist will have the following responsibilities:
Build, implement, manage, and support data pipeline and reporting automation solutions
Delivers process improvements to existing data processing and operational processes across the department
Works with the Data Scientist on high profile Operations Transformational projects
Provides analytical support for and insight into large scale big-data projects
Is responsible for end to end data projects
Provides analytical solutions for Product Owners
Manage ad hoc analysis requests, preparation and presentation of insights leveraging wider quality teams' expertise to help answer business stakeholder questions and support their objectives.
Develops dashboards for monitoring performance and quality across the different panels and processes

Your Skills & Experience

The perfect Junior Data Scientist will have:
Multiple years of experience analysing high volume disaggregated data is essential
Experience working as an analyst, data processor or data scientist in a commercial or academic capacity
Knowledge of foundational data analysis methods: data cleaning, summary statistics, validation, handling outliers, data visualisation
Ability to write complex SQL queries in at least one SQL variant (Oracle, MS Server) and deep understanding of aggregate functions, joins, subqueries
You have an analytical background and recent experience in business intelligence (Power BI preferred) and data storytelling (visual communication, dashboard design)
Experience with standard Python Machine Learning and data analysis stack (Pandas, NumPy, SciPy, Scikit-learn, etc.)
Understanding of how and when to apply predictive and machine learning techniques
Degree level or higher in a STEM subject

The Benefits

The successful Junior Data Scientist will receive a salary of up to £40,000 based upon on experience. On top of this the successful candidate will receive a great holiday allowance, a fantastic working culture, as well as other benefits.

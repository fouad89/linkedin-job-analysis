About The Job

As a Associate Data Analyst, you will be part of the Sellside Global Operations team, which supports publishers and helps drive accelerated business growth for them through our publisher result and platform solutions.

The Global Partnership Sales Engine (GPSE) Team is an integral part of creating efficiency and scale in support of our publisher result and platform solutions. The GPSE team is designed to accelerate operational activities and centralize work (e.g. operational activities like reporting, implementation, and optimization) away from sales teams so that front-line business leaders can concentrate their efforts on client-specific activities and continue growing a global business.

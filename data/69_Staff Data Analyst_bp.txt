Job Profile Summary

The Governance Risk and Compliance portfolio sits within Digital Security which is part of the Innovation and Engineering entity in bp. The GRC purpose is to provide bp with the capability to manage Cyber, IT and Compliance risks and sustain shareholder value.

Digital Security Measurement & Reporting is a new service within the Governance, Risk and Compliance portfolio. Our goal is to build and enable a consistent and measurable view of bpâ€™s Cyber and IT controls posture, underpinned by automation and data. The aim is to provide data driven insights through assessments, analytics and reporting that improves awareness and decision making capability across the business from mid to board level management.
Job Advert

The Senior Data Analyst will take a lead role in the M&R team and work closely with our customers, stakeholders and data teams. They will be responsible for creating consistent and easy to consume views of Cyber and IT Control metrics through data analytics and reporting. They will support the team in delivering bp LT and board reporting in a format that provides the appropriate business insights. They will provide governance of the metrics to make sure the sources are correct and consistently represented across the organization.

The Senior Performance Analyst will also work towards building our capability through making recommendations on leveraging what we already have in place to avoid duplication, best practices, working across bp teams and using existing or new tools to meet our business.

Job Summary



The primary role of a Data Analyst assisting our Financial Lines Broking business is to support our client focused business through comprehensive requirement analysis; detailed and accurate preparation of varying data; identification, analysis and interpretation of patterns in complex datasets and creation of compelling, creative and informative visualisation that supports decision-making for our clients and partners. Working closely alongside our Colleagues within HX, the Data Analyst plays a critical role in our evolving eco-system.



The ideal candidate will have a statistical, mathematical or data science-related background, a driven individual looking for the next step in their career, wanting to build on their foundational experience in data analytics to become a true practitioner. They will be confident in working with a variety of data sources as well as being able to adapt for technical and non-technical audiences.



As a growing, dynamic, and fast-paced company this role will suit someone who is looking to make an impact, and develop and shape a data analytics proposition. This is a superb opportunity to build a diverse set of skills in an environment at the cutting-edge of our industry, with exposure to different business areas, senior management, and clients.



Data Analysts are required to possess a range of finely-tuned data skills (data querying, data manipulation, data verification and visualisation), superb attention to detail as well as strong written and verbal communication skills.



We are committed to nurturing talent; to this end success in this role could lead to a transition into a range of other technical data or consulting-focussed roles.

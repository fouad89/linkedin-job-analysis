How You Can Make An Impact

As a Data Engineer on our team, you will manage a variety of datasets and data pipelines for traders, researchers, and other critical departments across the organization. You will use modern technologies to deliver high-volume data in near-real-time and manage our solution to measure/monitor the performance of our applications. In addition, you will build dashboards to provide real-time insights and help guide the next iteration of application development with usage statistics.
Design metrics, usage statics per application, work with application developers on instrumentation
Build Splunk dashboards, alerts, data models, and reports
Design and develop analytics solutions with traders and researchers
Design and develop data solutions with application developers
Manage data pipeline components to deliver high-volume, near-real-time data to various teams
Manage our Splunk installation and data pipeline for centralized log and metrics collection
Manage best-practice guidelines for logging and metrics 
Provide technical support to data consumers

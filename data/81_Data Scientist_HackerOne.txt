This is an exciting time to join us as a data scientist. We are using the latest tools and techniques to evolve our business intelligence platform and to achieve our objective of data democratization across the company.

You will have a point of view on data science best practices and will play a key role in the evolution and implementation of our strategy. You will work with analysts and stakeholders across the company on internal and customer focussed topics and must be curious, collaborative and analytical.

Mission of the BI Team at HackerOne

Data & analytics is key to what we do at HackerOne, and our BI team brings deep analytical, technical, partnering and consulting skills to help our customers and internal teams understand the success, efficiency and effectiveness of our customers programs and our internal operations.

Your Journey at HackerOne
Partner with stakeholders across the company (customer success, community, sales, marketing, finance and product) to establish and document data requirements
Own business KPIs, monitoring on-going applicability and relevance as we continue to grow and develop our business
Champion the overall data & BI strategy at HackerOne by sharing knowledge, techniques and standards
Identify, document and promote data analytics best practices
Establish operating processes to ensure operational excellence and openness
Introduce standard methodologies and principles around analytics and governance to enable consistent delivery and alignment with the strategic direction
Work with our product and engineering organizations to transform business requirements into architecture and integrate data into our longer term platform strategy

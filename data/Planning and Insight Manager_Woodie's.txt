Role Purpose:
Leading the delivery of our Planning and Insights strategy.
Key Responsibilities:
Lead the planning process to inform the overall commercial and marketing plan; setting up the processes and structures to advise brand, product and promotional strategies and customer communications.
Lead customer insights generation, defining key overall trends using outstanding research methodologies.
Use market research to encourage action across the business by delivering strategic and tactical insights.
Work closely with our Data Scientist to manage customer segmentation on an ongoing basis, crafting personas, adding to the understanding of our customer base.
Provide outstanding marketing and performance management analysis to track return on investment.

Marketing / Digital Qualification (Degree/Masters preferred) with at least 5 years proven experience in planning, strategy or project management with superb knowledge and experience of research and planning tools and approaches.
